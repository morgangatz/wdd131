Group Information

ATEEZ was created through one Kim Hongjoong. The future leader sent a letter and mixtape to KQ in 2016 expressing an interest in joining as a trainee. He became the first official trainee at the company, with Yunho joining him six months later. The rest of the members would join later, with a few coming from different companies, Yeosang and Wooyoung from BigHit, Jongho from TOP Media, and Mingi from Maroo Entertainment.

Debut: The group debuted October 24th, 2018 with EP "TREASURE EP1: All to Zero"

Group Name: ATEEZ is an acronym for "A TEEnager Z", It also stands for "A to Z" meaning they are a group of teenagers who are aiming to do everything they can enthusiastically.

Fandom Name: "ATINY" (pronouced as "a"-teen-"e" quotes mean you say the letter) ATINY is a combination of ATEEZ and DESTINY.

Company: KQ Entertainment
