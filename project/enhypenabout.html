Group Information

ENHYPEN was formed through the music survival show "I-Land". The group consists of seven members. Jungwon, Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, and Ni-ki.

Debut: The group debuted on November 30th, 2020, with the EP "Border: Day One".

Group Name: ENHYPEN derives their name from the hyphen symbol (-), representing "Connection, Discovery, & Growth". Similar to how a hyphen connects different words to create new meanings, Enhypen aims "to come together to connect, discover and grow together to form a new act".

Fandom Name: "ENGENE" (pronouced like the word "engine") "Because of the pronunciation of the word "ENGENE" the first thing it means is that, "[our] fans are like 'engines' that let [us] grow and keep going."[1] The second meaning is that the fans are "ENHYPEN's 'gene'" and that "[you] and ENHYPEN share the same DNA to connect, discover, and grow together." (Quote taken from this video)

Company: ENHYPEN is under the company BE:LIFT LAB
