Group Information

Stray Kids was created through the survival program with the same name. The group consists of eight members, who are Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N.

Debut: The group debuted March 25th, 2018 with EP "I Am Not"

Group Name: The name "Stray Kids" originally referred to a lost child who wants to chase their dreams and later evolved to represent the idea of finding a way together out of the ordinary.

Fandom Name: "STAY" The reason for choosing that name is because the members want their fans to always be by their side.

Company: JYP Entertainment
